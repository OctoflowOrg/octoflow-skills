# Delivery tracks (full detail)

Two tracks. Both include bank-grade hosting, guaranteed uptime,
complete orchestration of the automated system, and pass-through (no
markup) AI compute costs.

## Track 1 — Managed Micro-Automations

**$500/month + compute.** For standardized, tightly scoped workflows.

- Up to 3 specialized AI agents (routine research, daily reporting,
  intelligent task management)
- One automated evaluation loop (e.g., email categorization, document
  QA)
- One standard integration into the customer's existing software
- 1 hour of dedicated technical support and workflow adjustments per
  month
- Engagement starts with a 30-minute discovery call to map the
  workflow and scope first automations

## Track 2 — Custom Data & AI Architecture

**Starting at $1,500/month + compute.** For operations that fall
outside Track 1 — complex, proprietary, or regulated data; deeper
discovery; external service integration; multiple approval layers.

- Phase 1: fixed-price 4-hour workflow audit. Map technical
  requirements, regulatory constraints, internal business logic;
  define success criteria before any architecture is scoped.
- Phase 2: build in short milestone-validated iterations. Scope
  changes are surfaced and re-priced before new work begins —
  pricing transparent start to finish.
- Engineers scoping the system are the engineers building it.

## Add-ons (both tracks)

- **Support blocks**: 5 hours prepaid at $1,250 USD. Used for
  strategic maintenance, additional support, or custom modifications
  beyond a track's included allocation.
- **AI compute**: pass-through, paid directly by the customer at
  consumption rates. No OctoSync markup.

## Founding Partner offer (current cohort, three slots)

Time-limited promo, not a research filter — included here only
because it's part of how we price right now. Each founding partner
receives:

- Waived setup & deployment fees ($1,500 value)
- A 5-hour onboarding support block valid for the first 60 days
  ($1,250 value)
- Direct principal architect access

## How to use this reference

When the Strategist recommends an opportunity, they pick which track
fits — Track 1 if the workflow is standardized + tight scope (the
common case for the $1–1.5K/mo budget band); Track 2 if the
prospect's data is proprietary or regulated enough to require Phase 1
discovery. Avoid Track 2 for prospects who balk at the $1,500
baseline — Track 1 still serves the canonical OctoSync ICP.
