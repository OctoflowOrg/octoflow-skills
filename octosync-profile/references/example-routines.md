# Recognizable workflow shapes

These are real example routines from the OctoSync Capabilities Deck.
Useful mental models for "what this looks like in the wild" — **not a
menu**. The actual scope per customer is defined in their Phase 1
audit (Track 2) or 30-minute discovery call (Track 1).

- **Inbound lead triage** — enrich leads with firmographics, score
  against your ICP, route to the right rep.
- **Invoice processing** — extract line items from emailed invoices,
  match to open POs, flag exceptions.
- **Support ticket triage** — classify tickets, draft replies from
  knowledge base, escalate with a summary.
- **Weekly ops reporting** — Monday 8am: metrics from CRM, finance,
  and analytics compiled into a Slack report.
- **CRM hygiene** — weekly sweep for stale records, duplicates, and
  missing fields, fixed in batches.
- **Meeting follow-up** — transcribe calls, extract decisions and
  action items, email a recap to attendees.

These automations reach out across email, Slack/Teams, SMS, calendar,
dashboards, and webhooks/APIs into existing tools.

## How to use this reference

When the Strategist needs concrete examples to ground a
recommendation, or the LinkedIn Drafter needs a specific shape to
illustrate what OctoSync does, point at one of these. Always pair
with the caveat "these are starting points, not a menu — actual
scope is defined in discovery." Do not invent example shapes that
aren't on this list (or in actual customer history that the agent
can cite).
